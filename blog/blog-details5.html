PM mental models

Feb 22, 2023

PM mental models are conceptual frameworks that product managers use to understand and navigate the complexities of their roles. These mental models help PMs make informed decisions, anticipate challenges, and develop effective strategies. They encompass various aspects of product management, including understanding user needs, market dynamics, technical constraints, and business goals. By leveraging PM mental models, product managers can develop a holistic perspective and make data-driven decisions that contribute to the success of their products.
