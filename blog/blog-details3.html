Climate change impacts

Feb 28, 2023

Climate change is having a profound and far-reaching impact on our planet.** Rising global temperatures are causing more frequent and intense heatwaves, droughts, floods, and storms. These extreme weather events disrupt ecosystems, threaten food security, and displace communities. Additionally, climate change is contributing to the melting of glaciers and ice caps, leading to rising sea levels and coastal erosion. This poses significant risks to coastal cities and low-lying areas, putting millions of people at risk. Moreover, climate change is altering precipitation patterns, affecting agricultural productivity and water availability. The consequences of climate change are multifaceted and interconnected, requiring urgent action to mitigate its effects and build resilience.
